'''Vadyvokia Castle''' is an imposing, gothic castle overlooking the city of [[clu:Vadyvostadt|]]
The castle is the seat of government for the country of [[clu:Vadyvokia|]] and home to its head
of state, [[clu:Magnus Merlyn Malign|Marquis Malign]].
