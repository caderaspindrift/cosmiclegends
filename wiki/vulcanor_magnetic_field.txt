The '''Vulcanor magnetic field''', also known as the '''cloaking field''', was an energy field
created by Vulcanor scientists in the 9th Century B.C.E. that was operational until [[clu:1957|1957]].
The cloaking field served to protect the [[clu:Vulcanor archipelago|Vulcanor archipelago]] from detection by both
Earth civilizations and the [[clu:Visitors|Visitors]].

It was generated by the [[clu:Magnetic field tower|magnetic field tower]] in [[clu:Vulcanor palace|Vulcanor palace]]
on [[clu:Volcano island|Volcano Island]].
