'''Earth''', also known as ''W'Ty'Dah'k'' in [[clu:Titanian (language)|]], is the third planet in
the [[clu:solar system|]].

== Native Species ==

The dominant intelligent species on Earth is [[clu:Humans|humanity]], i.e. ''[[:w:Homo sapiens|Homo sapiens sapiens]]''.

Two other subspecies of humans exist, both the result of genetic manipulation by the [[clu:Visitors]]:

* The [[clu:Vulcanors|Vulcanors]] (''Homo sapiens vulcanis''); and
* The [[clu:Foreigners|Foreigners) (''Homo sapiens peregrinus'')
