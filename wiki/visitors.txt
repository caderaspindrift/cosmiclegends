The '''Visitors''' are a powerful and enigmatic alien race that has visited the [[clu:solar system|solar system]] three times.

The Visitors were responsible for the creation of the [[clu:Titanian (species)|Titanians]], [[clu:Vulcanor (species)|Vulcanors]],
and [[clu:Foreigner (species)|Foreigners]].
