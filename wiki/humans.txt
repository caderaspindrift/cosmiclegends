'''Humans''', also known as ''Homo sapiens'', and ''W'Ty'Okk'' in [[clu:Titanian (language)|Titanian]], are the
dominant species on the planet [[clu:Earth|Earth]].

== Subspecies ==

=== Current Subspecies ===

There are three modern subspecies of humanity:

* ''Homo sapiens sapiens''
* ''[[clu:Vulcanor (species)|Homo sapiens vulcanis]]''
* ''[[clu:Foreigner (species)|Homo sapiens peregrinus]]''

The latter two were created due to genetic tampering with ''Homo sapiens sapiens'' by the [[clu:Visitors|Visitors]].

=== Extinct Subspecies ===

21st-Century science has identified three subspecies of archaic humans that no longer exist on the Earth:

* ''[[:w:Herto_Man|Homo sapiens idaltu]]''
* ''[[:w:Neanderthal|Homo sapiens neanderthalensis]]''
* ''[[:w:Homo_heidelbergensis|Homo sapiens heidelbergensis]]''
